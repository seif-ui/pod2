System Documentation

How the Pod 2 system works — processes, cycles, and standards.

Client Onboarding Process (7 Days)

PHASE 1: DIAGNOSTIC
Day 1-2: All 4 team members conduct their briefs with the client. Seif + Raneem do welcome meeting. Each person asks their specific onboarding questions.
PHASE 2: BUILD
Day 3-4: Each person builds their deliverables from brief insights. Seif: strategy. Ziad: creative plan. Jimmy: media plan. Raneem: operations setup. Day 4: present to client.
PHASE 3: EXECUTE
Day 5-7: Tasks distributed. Content production starts. Ads prepared and launched. Day 7: GO LIVE — first content published, first ads live.

WEEK 4

Premium Service Model

We are not just marketers. Premium is a brandformance agency — we combine brand building with performance marketing. Every client gets:

Required Basics: Strategy, content plan, paid ads management, operations setup, weekly reporting, monthly reporting.
Extra Retention: Competitor intelligence, trend research, creative testing, CRO audits, loyalty programs, brand architecture — varies per client needs.

Key Principle: Every team member has full freedom to contribute across all functions. There are no silos. If Ziad sees a performance issue, he flags it. If Jimmy has a creative idea, he shares it. If Raneem spots a strategy gap, she raises it. The client's success is everyone's responsibility.
